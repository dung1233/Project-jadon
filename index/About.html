About US

Jadon Manager wedding production is brought to you by a highly creative and motivated team.

For well over a decade, we have been organising bespoke corporate and private events, ranging from intimate, subtle and refined, to outrageously extravagant! Every event is made unique and unforgettable with a sprinkling of JD imagination and creativity. London provides the base for us to work throughout the UK and anywhere in the world. Our team will travel to any place of your desire and throw a party well beyond your dreams.

Together you may have contemplated your wedding down to the finest detail, or perhaps you are just starting out with a fresh canvas, looking for inspiration. No matter what phase you have reached in your planning, our stage design services will prove to be a valuable resource, as your dedicated production manager works closely with you to achieve the most stunning, breath-taking wedding stage design you could possibly envisage. You could opt for bespoke staging, which is available should you wish for your own exclusive stage design service. If you would like to arrange a personal meeting, or have questions you would like answered, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Our clients include: BP, NetApp, Deloitte, Ernst & Young, French Connection, Immediate Media and Vitol, together with leading law firms: Pinsent Masons, Sheridans, Penningtons Manches LLP, Plexus Law. The charity Global Angels and renowned restaurants: Sushisamba, D&D Restaurant Group, Caprice Holding. To get a better idea of the services we provide, take a look through our photo gallery and press releases, where you will see a sample of what Beyond Certainty event management services can do. Contact us with any query or for a free no obligation meeting.
